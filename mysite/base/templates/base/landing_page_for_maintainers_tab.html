Tips on finding more contributors

Most projects have only one active developer. How can you go from zero collaborators to more? Here are our tips:

  • Find local colleges and universities with computer clubs or computer science departments. Email them and tell them how cool your project is.
  • Give a talk at a local programming language meetup about your project.
  • Run an online event to attract contributors. Read more on our wiki.
  • Join #openhatch on irc.freenode.net and see if we have other ideas.

Contact us. We can help, and give you ideas »
