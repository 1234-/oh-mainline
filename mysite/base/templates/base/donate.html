Donate to OpenHatch!

OpenHatch is a non-profit that maintains this website, organizes outreach events to grow the open source community, and brings diversity to programming groups.

We depend on donations from companies and individuals to continue these efforts. If you are able, we deeply appreciate any donation you can make.

$

If you are willing to try something unusual, you can also donate to us using Dwolla or a check in the mail and save us some fees. Contact us to arrange non-PayPal donations or to share your ideas for helping support OpenHatch.

Sponsoring Open Source Comes to Campus, for Women in Computing

We're still looking for companies and individuals that are excited about our efforts to teach students how to get involved in open source. This year, in 2013, we are focusing on bringing those workshops to women in computer science groups, aiming to narrow the gender gap in open source.

Please read our sponsor prospectus (PDF) and get in touch by emailing hello@openhatch.org!

Sponsoring OpenHatch as a whole

If you believe in our mission generally, and are enthusiastic about sponsoring the organization, please send an email to hello@openhatch.org!
